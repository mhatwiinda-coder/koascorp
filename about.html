About KOAS

A Zambian-owned company built on integrity, reliability, and industry-focused supply solutions.

Who We Are

KOAS Corporation Limited is a fully registered Zambian company specializing in paint manufacturing, mine supply, and general dealership services.

We serve industrial, mining, and commercial clients with a strong focus on quality, compliance, and long-term partnerships.

Our Vision

To be a trusted and preferred supplier of industrial and mining solutions in Zambia and the region.

Our Mission

To deliver quality products and dependable services while upholding integrity, compliance, and operational excellence.
